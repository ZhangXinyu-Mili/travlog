How Points Work

Each achievement you complete earns you 10 points. As you accumulate points, you'll automatically advance through different tier levels, unlocking new recognition.

Advancement Levels

🥉
Bronze Tier
Unlocked at 10+ points (1+ achievements)
🥈
Silver Tier
Unlocked at 30+ points (3+ achievements)
🥇
Gold Tier
Unlocked at 50+ points (5+ achievements)
💎
Diamond Tier
Unlocked at 100+ points (10+ achievements)

Note: Your tier level is determined by your total accumulated points. Once you reach a tier, you maintain that status as you continue earning more achievements.
